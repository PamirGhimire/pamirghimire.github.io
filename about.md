I am Pamir, a software engineer with more than seven years of experience in the automotive industry. My work centers on modern C++, software architecture, system integration, and building production software for complex technical systems.

My route into the field began with electronics, computer vision, robotics, and applied research. Those early projects still influence how I think about software: as part of a wider system with physical constraints, imperfect inputs, and real consequences when interfaces are unclear.

I write about software engineering, C++, APIs, build systems, and the trade-offs involved in turning technical ideas into maintainable systems. I also maintain an interest in climate change, demographics, and supply chains.
